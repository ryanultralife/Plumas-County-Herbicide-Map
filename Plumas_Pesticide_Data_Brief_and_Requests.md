# Plumas NF / Plumas County — Pesticide & Herbicide Data: What We Have, What to Pull, What to Request

**Scope:** USFS Plumas National Forest footprint (Plumas, Butte, Sierra, Lassen, Yuba counties) **plus** Plumas County private land. Activities **completed and planned**, 2020–present. Compiled June 2026.

---

## The core insight

Your existing map is built from **California Pesticide Use Reporting (PUR)** data — that covers **private land only** (Sierra Pacific Industries, Collins Pine, W.M. Beaty, ranches). It does *not* include the **federal Plumas National Forest itself**. The federal side lives in entirely separate systems, and it splits in two:

- **"What & where" (public, downloadable now):** FACTS activity polygons, the SOPA planned-actions schedule, and NEPA project documents. These give treatment acres, locations, dates, and named herbicides in project decisions.
- **"Which chemical & how much" (FOIA only):** the Forest Service's internal **Pesticide-Use Proposals (FS-2100-2)** and regional use reports. The agency has publicly stated that detailed application numbers require a records request.

So: we can pull a lot today, and there are exactly two things worth formally requesting — the **federal chemical-level data (FOIA to Region 5)** and the **most-recent local private data (county Ag Commissioner)**.

---

## What we can pull right now (public)

1. **CalPIP / DPR PUR** — refresh your private-land data: add **2020 and 2025**, and pull **all five counties**, not just Plumas. https://calpip.cdpr.ca.gov/main.cfm
2. **FACTS (FSGeodata Clearinghouse)** — download the treatment-polygon geodatabases (Hazardous Fuel Treatments, Timber Harvests, Range Vegetation Improvement, Common Attributes), clip to Plumas NF (Admin Forest 0511). This is the best public federal layer for the map. https://data.fs.usda.gov/geodata/edw/datasets.php
3. **SOPA** — every quarterly Schedule of Proposed Actions 2020→present; extract herbicide/vegetation projects. https://www.fs.usda.gov/sopa/components/reports/sopa-110511-2025-07.pdf (change the date in the URL per quarter)
4. **NEPA project pages** — harvest all vegetation/herbicide projects, prioritizing the **Community Protection Project (CPP)**. https://www.fs.usda.gov/project/plumas/?project=62873
5. **CalFire CalTREES / Timber Harvest Viewer** — Timber Harvest Plans often name site-prep/release herbicides on private timberland. https://caltreesplans.resources.ca.gov/caltrees/

## What we must request

| Request | To whom | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Federal pesticide-use data (PUPs + use reports, 2020–present) | **USFS Region 5 FOIA** — SM.FS.R5FOIA@usda.gov | Only way to get chemical/quantity on NF land |
| Most-recent local NOIs & permits | **Plumas County Ag Commissioner** | Captures the current season before it hits CalPIP |

The full source matrix, the CPP detail sheet, and the contact list are in **`Plumas_Pesticide_Data_Source_Inventory.xlsx`**.

## DRAFT 1 — FOIA request to USFS Region 5 (ready to send)

> **To:** SM.FS.R5FOIA@usda.gov
> **Subject:** FOIA Request — Plumas National Forest pesticide/herbicide use records, 2020–present

To the Region 5 FOIA Officer,

Under the Freedom of Information Act, 5 U.S.C. § 552, I request copies of the following records concerning the **Plumas National Forest** (all ranger districts) for the period **January 1, 2020 to the present**:

1. All **Pesticide-Use Proposals (Form FS-2100-2 / "PUP")** approved, submitted, or implemented for the Plumas National Forest, including the active ingredient(s), product name/EPA registration number, target pest, treatment acreage, application method, total quantity applied, and treatment location.
2. Any **annual or periodic pesticide/herbicide use reports or summaries** compiled at the Forest or Region 5 level that include Plumas National Forest applications (e.g., active-ingredient totals and acres treated by year).
3. Any **pesticide application records, daily use logs, or contractor application reports** for herbicide treatments carried out on the Plumas National Forest.
4. Records sufficient to show **planned/proposed** pesticide use for ongoing projects, including the Community Protection Project.

Please provide records in electronic format (searchable PDF, spreadsheet, or native file) where available. I request a fee waiver as disclosure is in the public interest and not for commercial use; if the request is denied, please notify me before incurring costs exceeding $25.

Please confirm receipt and the assigned tracking number. Thank you.

[Name / address / email / phone]
